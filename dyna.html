Zambia

Zambia officially the Republic of Zambia is a landlocked country at the crossroad of central southern and eastern Africa.It is typically reffered to being a south central Africa.It is bordered to the north by Democratic Republic of Congo, Tanzania to the north east,Malawi to the east, Mozambique to the south east,Zimbabwe and Botswana to the south, Namibia to the south west and Angola to the west. The capital city of Zambia is Lusaka, located in the south central part of Zambia.The population is concentrated mainly around Lusaka in the south and the Copperbelt province to the north, the core economic hubs of the country. Originary inhibited by khoisan people,the region which was affected by the Bantu expansion of the 13th century. Following European explorers in the 18th century,the British protectorate of Barotziland Northern_Western Rhodesia and Northern_Eastern Rhodesia to words the end of 19th century.These were merged in 19th to form Northern Rhodesia. For most of colonial period, Zambia was governed by an administration appointed from London with the advice of the British South campany. On 24th october 1964, Zambia become independant of the United Kingdom and prime minister Kenneth Kaunda become inaugural President. Kaunda as the first Socialist United Nation Independence Part(UNIP) maintained power from 1964 until 1991. From 1972 to 1991 Zambia was single party state with the UNIP as the so legal political part with the goal of uniting the nation under the banner of 'ONE ZAMBIA ONE Nation'. Kaunda was successeded by Dr Fredric Chiluba of the movement for multiple_party democracy in 1991.Dr Fredric Chiluba selected Mr Leavy Mwanawasa presided over the the countryfrom january 2002 until his death in Augast 2008,and is credited with initiating a campaign to reduce corruption and increase the standard of living . After Mwanawasa`s death, Mr Rupiah Banda presided as Acting President before being elected President in 2008.He is the shortest serving President, having held office for only three years . Patriotic party leader, His Excellence Mr Michael Chilufya Sata defeated Mr Banda in 2011 election. His Excellence Mr Hakainde Hichilema a bussiness man, farmer and a politician who is a seventh and current President of Zambia since 24 Augast 2021 after having contested five previuos election in 2006,2008,2011,2015 and 2016, he won the 2021 presidential election with 59.02% of the votes. Priol to His election , Hichilema was a major opponet of Mr Edger chagwa Lungu, The former president of zambia from 2015 to 2021.Mr Hichilema Hakainde was arrested and charged with treason a move that was seen as an illigitimate act by Lungu to silence political rival. Today, zambia is a Democratic country with growing economy,rich in natural resources, and a diverse culture.
